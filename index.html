Physics and process as the true regulators.

An 11‑move grammar + seven open indices for policy action.

The Grammar (11 moves)

  • 1. Hidden Conservation Law: What flows in ≠ what flows out because an invisible stock is accumulating.
  • 2. Horizon Mismatch: Decision horizon ≪ constraint horizon; optimization at short horizons creates long‑horizon failure.
  • 3. Illegibility Premium: Opacity is not a bug; it is the rent‑extraction feature.
  • 4. Metric Collapse: The proxy eats the un‑measured dimension (Goodhart/Campbell).
  • 5. Transparency Arbitrage: Mandatory disclosure flips information asymmetry against the discloser.
  • 6. Coordination Number Collapse: >150 nodes ⇒ worse coordination despite more links (layering, cliques, overload).
  • 7. Shame‑to‑Anonymity Conversion: Local reputational constraints are removed by delocalized capital + legal shielding.
  • 8. Latency as Currency: Physics (c) creates geographic rent that cannot be arbitraged away.
  • 9. Scarcity‑Created Power: Under‑resourcing increases discretionary power and preemptive compliance.
  • 10. Lock‑In via Openness: Open standards and integrations can create higher switching costs than proprietary control.
  • 11. Phase‑Transition Stranding: Assets don’t decay; they snap when thresholds are crossed (tech/regulation/physics).

Open Indices

  1. TDAR — Thermal Debt Accumulation Rate — TDAR = (Heat_in − Dissipation_cap) × Time / Local_thermal_capacity.
  2. IBCI — Interconnect Bandwidth Constraint Index — IBCI = (Compute_CAGR ÷ Interconnect_CAGR) × (Lead‑time_factor ÷ BW_threshold).
  3. RLR — Regulatory Leverage Ratio — RLR = (Enforcement_discretion × Penalty_severity) / (Staff_resources × Procedural_constraints).
  4. RTAW — Regulatory Transition Arbitrage Window — RTAW = Enforcement_go‑live_date − Announcement_date (in months), adjusted for sector variants.
  5. TEI — Transparency Exploitation Index — TEI increases when disclosures enable coordination/targeting (vs consumer benefit).
  6. ACEI — Algorithmic Collusion Emergence Index — ACEI predicts tacit price alignment absent explicit agreement (RL pricing, shared vendors).
  7. BOOI — Beneficial Ownership Opacity Index — BOOI measures the gap between legal beneficial‑ownership visibility and effective control.
